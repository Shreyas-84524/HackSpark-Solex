Illuminating the Future of Solar Finance
Overview : Solex is a B2B SaaS platform designed to streamline financial modeling and ROI analysis for commercial solar installations.
We take the complexity out of energy forecasting, tax incentive calculations, and seasonal efficiency metrics, allowing solar developers and financiers to close deals faster.
Key Features
Dynamic ROI Calculator: Calculate project payback periods instantly based on location, panel efficiency, and local energy rates.
Seasonal Modeling: Accurately simulate energy production fluctuations using historical weather data (kWh output over time).
Investor Dashboards: Generate professional, data-driven reports for stakeholders in a single click.
